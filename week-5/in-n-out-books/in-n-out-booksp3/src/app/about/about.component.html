Chuck Palahniuk

Chuck Palahniuk was born in 1962 in Burbank, Washington. The surname is of Ukrainian origin and his family life was less than stellar. His paternal grandfather shot his wife before turning the gun on himself when his father was three, and Chuck’s father died by gunshot as well from a lover’s ex-partner later in life. Chuck credits his interest in writing to his fifth-grade teacher, Mr. Olsen, and incorporates much of his own life experience into his writings. While the beginning of his career did not start with a bang, Chuck’s success includes New York Times bestseller Choke, as well as a movie adaptation of his book, Fight Club.
